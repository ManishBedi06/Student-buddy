Student Buddy

A simple guide for new students in Comox Valley.

What is Student Buddy?

Student Buddy is a student-made website created to help new and international students at Comox Valley. Starting days are so confusing, especially if you are new to the area. This website brings important information together in one place and explains it in simple language so students can feel more confident during their first weeks.

Who Is This Guide For?

  • New students starting at NIC
  • International students coming to Comox Valley
  • Students who feel confused or unsure
  • Students looking for simple and clear information

Why Use Student Buddy?

Many students feel lost at the beginning of college. Information is often spread across different websites and can be hard to understand. Student Buddy is made to save time, reduce stress, and help students find answers quickly in one place.
